I’m drawn to hard problems where science, markets, and execution intersect.

I’m a PhD chemist with experience across scientific research, technology commercialization, startups, and early-stage investing. I’m interested in how complex scientific ideas become useful tools, products, companies, and investment opportunities.

My research has spanned stem cell differentiation, intrinsically disordered proteins, liquid-liquid phase separation, protein aggregation, and method development at different stages. Across these problems, I’ve used wet-lab experiments, nonlinear spectroscopy, and computational analysis and modeling to make complex biological systems more measurable and interpretable.
