Consulting

I provide strategic consulting for life-science startups, venture firms, and family offices. My goal is to help founders navigate biotech innovation and investment with practical, hands-on support from concept to financing to preclinical execution. Please don’t hesitate to contact me!

How I help

  • Diligence & landscape: technical validation, competitive/IP analysis, risk mapping
  • Non-dilutive funding: SBIR/STTR authorship, Specific Aims, Research Strategy, timelines
  • R&D roadmaps: preclinical study design, CRO coordination, data QC
  • Go-to-market & BD: indication assessment, KOL interviews, partnerships
  • Financials: DCF analysis, bottoms-up models, cap-table analysis
  • Investor materials: narrative, deck, data room, outreach strategy
  • Operator support: project management and creative ad-hoc help
